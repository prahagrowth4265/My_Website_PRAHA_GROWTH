Refund & Cancellation Policy

At Praha Growth, we strive to provide high-quality digital services and complete transparency in our work. This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines the terms regarding project cancellations and refunds for services provided by our agency.

1. Services Covered

Our services include but are not limited to:

  • Brand Strategy
  • Website Design & Development
  • UI/UX Design
  • Digital Marketing
  • Social Media Marketing
  • Growth Strategy & Consulting

All services are delivered based on mutually agreed project scope, timelines, and payment terms.

2. Cancellation Policy

  • Clients may request project cancellation before work begins.
  • If a project is cancelled after work has started, charges will apply based on the work completed.
  • Once a project reaches advanced stages (design approval, development phase, or delivery stage), cancellation may not be possible.

3. Refund Policy

  • Advance payments made for project initiation are generally non-refundable.
  • If cancellation occurs before work begins, a partial refund may be considered after deducting administrative costs.
  • No refunds will be issued once design concepts, strategies, or development work has been delivered.
  • Refunds (if approved) will be processed within 7–10 business days.

4. Project Delays

Project timelines depend on client collaboration. Delays caused by lack of communication, missing materials, or late feedback may affect project delivery timelines and will not qualify for refunds.

5. Failed or Duplicate Payments

  • If a duplicate payment occurs, the extra amount will be refunded.
  • If a payment fails but funds are deducted from the client's account, the refund will be processed once confirmed by the payment provider.
